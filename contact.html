Frequently Asked Questions

How can I add my university to your platform?

Universities can partner with us by contacting our partnerships team at partnerships@uniselect.com. We'll guide you through the verification and listing process.

Is your service completely free for students?

Yes, all our university search and recommendation services are completely free for students. We may offer premium services in the future, but the core functionality will remain free.

How often is your university data updated?

We update our database quarterly, but critical information like admission deadlines is updated in real-time as we receive notifications from universities.
